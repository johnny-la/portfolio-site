The "Battle Circle"

Method

The "Mean Distance" Heuristic

To create a cinematic combat circle, a distance vector is calculated from the enemies' center of mass to the player's position, creating a movement target for the next enemy. The opponent then walks to the empty spot, creating a circular arrangement around the user.
